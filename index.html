ABOUT TRAVEL TOGETHER

Keep the details of your trip organized. View the group plans on one central calendar, create your own itinerary, then sync the relevant detilas with the group, such as arrival and departure times.

Things You Can Do

Build the Group Itinerary

Create the overall itinerary for the trip. You can grant permission to participants view, add comments, or make changes to your plans. You can also send notifications to your group members.

Build Your Own Itinerary

Add flight, hotel, and car rental information to your own itinerary. If you want to schedule something separate from the group, you can do it here. Then decide which details to sync with the group calendar.

Sync With Personal Calendars

Sync master and personal itineraries with Google Calendar, Outlook, or iCal.

Collect and Pay for Shared Expenses

Add costs for shared dinners, excursions, or whatever else you like, and decide how to split them with others.
